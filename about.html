Welcome to "Where to play Volleyball in London".

The aim for this page is to help volleyball players/coaches/clubs to find where to play in London. The first version of the Website provides list of known gyms that host, or could host, volleyball.

Contributing

The information here presented was gathered by different means and will need updates. Anyone interested in helping out correcting this information, please drop me an email and I will update the data presented.

Next Step

The next step for this page is to be able to look for actual teams in London by selecting the level of competition, the gender and the days the team trains. This would then help everyone find a club
